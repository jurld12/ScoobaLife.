Scooba Life


A well-established diving company. Scooba Life has grown to become the leading PADI certification and watersports specialist in the area. We offer PADI Scuba Diving Courses for beginners to the professional level. With a price for all budgets start your PADI scuba diving adventure from our dive center in Jumeirah and go on to explore the best dive sites in the Emirates. We are happy and ready to assist you!

Equipment
